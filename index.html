    What Happens Now?

    01

    Contact

    After you have filled in the contact form, a broker will contact you within 24 hours to schedule a short digital meeting.

    02

    Digital Meeting

    During the digital meeting, you and the broker will go through the entire rental process and the details of your apartment. You will also agree on times for photography and 3D scanning of your home.

    03

    Brokers Agreement

    After the digital meeting, a broker's agreement is sent to you for digital signature. The agreement grants Swedish Living the right to advertise your property.

    04

    3D Scanning & Photography

    One of our staff visits your home to take pictures and 3D scan your property. The 3D scan is a tool that facilitates the renting process by eliminating the need for physical viewings.

    05

    Advertising

    Once the photos are taken, the rent is set, and you have been verified as a landlord, we publish your property on our platform and other relevant sites to ensure comprehensive distribution.

    06

    Contact

    When a suitable tenant is found, an agreement for e-signing is sent. The tenant signs first and then the agreement is automatically forwarded to you, along with the credit report and the tenant's employment contract among other things.

    07

    Access & Inspection

    After the agreement has been signed by both parties, the key handover remains. We also guide you here by providing an inspection protocol and a move-in checklist. This ensures that everything goes smoothly when you hand over the property to the tenant.
